Education

With over 7 years experience of tutoring elementary, middle, high school, and college students, both in-person and online, I'm also working towards a minor in education with a focus on the secondary track of science and mathematics.
I currently serve as an Undergraduate Learning Assistant for the Department of Statistics and Applied Probability, in which I hold biweekly office hours, lead lab sections, and facilitate lectures alongside the professor to answer student questions.
As I strive to facilitate my students’ learning, my greatest priority as a tutor is to build rapport: I believe that a student is best able to learn in an environment where they feel comfortable enough to ask questions and make mistakes. My consistent efforts in building this rapport have successfully aided me in transforming the tutor-tutee relationship into friendships, ultimately encouraging students to attend office hours more regularly and become more proactive with their education.

Mental Health

Lean On Me

Through my involvement with Lean On Me UCSB, I had the opportunity to work in the data analytics team as a Data Visualization Intern for Lean On Me National in summer 2022. By analyzing over 6 years of existing temporal, binary, qualitative and quantitative data, I created visualizations using the ggplot2 and Plotly libraries under R, in order to display on the organization’s national as well as individual chapter websites. With this data, I drew conclusions and relationships through visualizing bar graphs, time series charts, and box-and-whisker plots to effectively communicate the texting services’ impact in its communities.
